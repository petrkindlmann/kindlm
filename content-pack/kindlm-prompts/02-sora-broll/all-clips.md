# Sora 2 B-roll Prompts

Paste each prompt directly into Sora 2 (via HeyGen integration or standalone app).
Settings: 1080p, 5-10 seconds each, cinematic style.


## CLIP 01: "Friday deploy disaster" (for launch video opening)

A developer sitting at a desk in a dimly lit office, looking stressed as red notification alerts appear on multiple monitors. The camera slowly pushes in. Late evening light through blinds. Cinematic, moody lighting, shallow depth of field. No text overlays.


## CLIP 02: "Code flowing" (general B-roll for tutorials)

Abstract visualization of lines of code flowing upward through a dark space, glowing in soft indigo and green light. The code transforms into structured data blocks that lock into place like puzzle pieces. Smooth camera movement, macro lens feel. Dark background, no text.


## CLIP 03: "Testing pipeline" (for CI/CD content)

A conveyor belt metaphor in a clean dark environment. Glowing cubes move along the belt, passing through checkpoints that flash green or red. One cube flashes red and is diverted. Mechanical, precise movement. Overhead camera angle, industrial-minimal aesthetic.


## CLIP 04: "Compliance document being stamped" (for EU AI Act videos)

A document on a dark desk illuminated by a single desk lamp. A digital stamp appears on the document with a satisfying press motion, leaving a glowing blue seal. The camera is close, shallow depth of field. Paper texture visible. Professional, authoritative mood.


## CLIP 05: "Two models racing" (for comparison videos)

Two glowing spheres, one blue and one green, racing along parallel tracks in a dark environment. They pulse as they process, leaving light trails. The blue sphere finishes first but the green sphere glows brighter at the finish line. Dynamic camera following the race. Abstract, no text.


## CLIP 06: "Agent making decisions" (for tool call content)

A luminous branching tree structure growing in real-time against a dark background. At each branch point, a small decision node glows and selects a path. Some paths glow green, one branch flashes amber. Organic growth movement, neural network aesthetic.


## CLIP 07: "Security scan" (for PII detection content)

A stream of text data flowing across a dark screen. A scanning beam moves across the text, highlighting certain patterns in red that then get redacted with a clean blur effect. Clinical, precise, security-focused aesthetic. Matrix-inspired but professional.


## CLIP 08: "Clock counting down" (for urgency/deadline content)

An analog clock face in extreme close-up, ticking. The hands approach a marked deadline point. As they get closer, the ticking gets more intense. Shallow depth of field, warm desk lamp lighting in background. Tension without being dramatic.


## CLIP 09: "Terminal in action" (aesthetic filler)

Extreme close-up of a high-resolution monitor showing a terminal with green and blue text scrolling. Shallow depth of field. The reflection of the text visible on the developer's glasses. Cinematic, atmospheric, late-night coding mood.


## CLIP 10: "Team reviewing results" (for enterprise content)

Three developers around a standing desk pointing at a dashboard showing test results. The dashboard has green checkmarks and one red alert. Natural office lighting, medium shot, casual but professional environment. No faces in focus, hands and screens emphasized.
